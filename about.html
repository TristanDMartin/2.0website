Hello, I’m Tristan

Hello! I'm a versatile Project Manager based in New Jersey, and I am actively seeking both full-time roles and freelance projects. My expertise lies in developing Front-End websites and creating engaging UX designs for a diverse range of companies, startups, and brands.

Currently, I am proud to be part of Sesame Communications, where I serve as a dedicated Project Manager. In this role, I lead a talented team of three designers and eight builders, collaborating closely with them to bring our members' websites to life. Together, we work tirelessly to deliver exceptional results that exceed expectations and create impactful online experiences.

My journey in the tech industry began with a focus on Full Stack Development, where I honed my skills through a rigorous coding Bootcamp at Rutgers University. Prior to that, I gained hands-on experience in the IT field, providing invaluable support to clients by maintaining their computer systems and resolving OS issues. This practical background has strengthened my technical expertise and shaped my problem-solving abilities.

When I'm not immersed in project management or coding, I enjoy exploring my creative side. I spend time streaming on Twitch, delving into video editing, and continually seeking opportunities to learn new skills that enhance my craft. This commitment to self-improvement drives me to deliver the highest quality work while staying at the forefront of industry trends.

I am passionate about collaborating with clients, leveraging my expertise to create impactful digital solutions that drive results. With a strong foundation in project management, web development, and UX design, I am ready to tackle new challenges and contribute to the success of your next project. Let's connect and bring your vision to life!
